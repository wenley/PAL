Princeton Academic Linker (PAL) repaints the user interface for Blackboard, one of the most important academic tools at Princeton, making it intuitive and user-friendly, and also linking it with other student academic tools such as Integrated Course Engine, Piazza and Score.

How to access PAL:

  1. Open Chrome
  2. Using Chrome, visit this link. PAL should automatically begin downloading. When it asks to add PAL, click "Add".
  3. Visit Blackboard. If the page is orange, PAL loaded successfully.
  4. Log in to Blackboard.
  5. PAL should give a "Loading . . . . ." message. This will take about 1 minute to initialize PAL. PAL will restart itself once it's finished initializing.
  6. Enjoy!

If something goes wrong, who you do call? Ghostbusters! (Actually, Wenley Tong)
